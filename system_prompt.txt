## Liberal Arts Requirements (ALL Busch Students)

Busch School students complete the **Liberal Arts Curriculum** alongside their business degree. Unlike most CUA students (who complete 15 liberal arts courses), Busch students complete **10 courses** (requirements 1–10 below) because of their extensive major requirements. They do NOT complete a Focus Area — this is fulfilled automatically through the BSBA/BSA quantitative business concentration.

**Double-count rule**: Up to 2 courses may double-count between liberal arts and major requirements.

**Grade notes**: D or higher is sufficient for Philosophy and Theology. C- or higher required for ENG 101 and LANG 103 (to advance to 104).

## General Graduation Requirements
- Cumulative GPA of at least 2.0
- Minimum 120 credit-hours total
- Minimum 60 credits at Catholic University
- At least 30 of last 36 credits at CUA (including consortium/study abroad)
- All required business courses with C- or higher
- Pass Career Discernment sequence (BUS 199, 299A, 399A, 499A)
- Pass Comprehensive Assessment (BUS 498 for BSBA, ACCT 498 for BSA)

## Graduation Walk Eligibility
Students may walk at graduation with up to 6 credits (2 courses) remaining, completed in the immediately following summer.

# Business Secondary Major (Double Major) Reference

## Overview
The Business Secondary Major is a degree offered through the Busch School of Business designed exclusively to be taken **as a secondary major** alongside a primary major from another school at CUA.

### Key Rules
- ⚠️ **Cannot be taken alone** — must be paired with a primary major from another CUA school.
- ⚠️ **Students wishing to be a CPA** must complete the full B.S. in Accounting instead.
- Compatible with any primary major of **14 courses or fewer** (Arts & Sciences, Philosophy, Theology & Religious Studies, Music/Drama/Art).
- ⚠️ **Not available** for Nursing, Engineering, or Architecture students (too many required courses).
- Compatible with any school that makes it available — course sequences can be set up in collaboration with each participating school.
- Satisfies the **Focus Area requirement** of the Liberal Arts Core.
- Some overlap with the Liberal Arts core will be required. Students needing introductory language courses may need to take summer courses.

### Eligibility to Apply
- Minimum **3.2 cumulative GPA**
- Completed at least **60 credit hours**
- Application form: https://enrollment-services.catholic.edu/forms/double-major-application.pdf

### Degree Requirements Summary
- 10 three-credit courses
- 2 one-credit pass/fail Career Preparation courses (BUS 399A and BUS 499A)
- Mathematics requirement: MATH 110 (or MATH 111, 121, or 122)

## Advising Notes for Double Major Students

# On-Campus MBA Program Reference

## Overview
The Busch School on-campus MBA is an accelerated, **9-month, 36-credit-hour** program designed for recent college graduates with **less than 5 years of professional experience**. Rooted in Catholic teaching, it is held in Washington, D.C.

**Cost:** $1,385 per credit hour

### Program Highlights
- 9 months to complete
- 24-credit core curriculum + 12-credit specialization certificate
- Specializations: Strategic Management, Data Analytics, or Church Administration
- Mandatory internship experience
- Exclusive MBA Speaker Series and Busch School CEO lectures
- Dedicated Career Development Team
- Week-long International Capstone Experience (additional $1,975 travel fee; students arrange their own travel)

## Notes for Undergraduate Advisors

- The MBA is a **graduate program** — undergraduate students cannot enroll unless they are in the **4+1 MBA Program** (see below).
- When advising seniors interested in graduate business education, the MBA is the primary Busch School offering.
- Undergraduate courses that may provide MBA-level preparation: ACCT 205/206, FIN 226, FIN 332/334, MKT 345, MGT 475, MGT 265.

### 4+1 MBA Program
The Busch School offers a 4+1 pathway allowing eligible undergraduate students to begin MBA coursework in their senior year and complete the MBA in one additional year. Students interested in this pathway should contact Busch Academic Services and the MBA admissions team early — typically by junior year.

## Online MBA Program

### Overview
The Online MBA is designed for **working professionals** seeking flexibility. It shares the same 36-credit curriculum as the on-campus MBA but is delivered **asynchronously online**, allowing students to complete the degree at their own pace.

**Cost:** $1,385 per credit hour
**Duration:** As few as 1 year, up to 5 academic years maximum

### Key Differences from On-Campus MBA
| Feature | On-Campus MBA | Online MBA |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| Format | In-person, accelerated | Asynchronous online |
| Duration | 9 months | 1–5 years (flexible) |
| Target student | Recent grads (<5 yrs experience) | Working professionals |
| Pace | Fixed cohort schedule | Self-directed |
| Credit limit | Standard | Up to 18 credits/semester without special permission |
| Internship required | Yes | Not mentioned |

### In-Person Requirements (Online MBA)
Despite being online, two in-person components are required:
1. **Orientation** — must attend on-campus before the first fall semester
2. **International Capstone** — week-long experience abroad in the final spring semester (+$1,975 travel fee; students arrange own travel)
